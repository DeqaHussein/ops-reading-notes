What does that even mean? How can the OSI model be used in troubleshooting? What Is Wireshark, and How Is It Used?
Hardware layers typically refer to the actual physical stuff, like cables, etc. Software layers are responsible for the management of the data. For troubleshooting, you can guess which layer is the troublemaker based on what is not working.
